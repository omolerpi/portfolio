Airbnb Dataset

Airbnb is a popular online platform that connects travelers with locals

• MS Excel • Tableau

Introduction

Airbnb is a popular online platform that connects travelers with local hosts who rent out their homes or apartments for short-term stays. In Seattle, Washington, Airbnb is a popular choice for visitors seeking affordable and unique accommodations. With over 6,000 listings, Airbnb offers a variety of options ranging from cozy apartments in downtown Seattle to scenic waterfront homes with stunning views of Puget Sound.



Data

To analyze how Airbnb operates in Seattle, Washington, I have gotten data from Kaggle. I will be discussing the prize by zip code, average price per bedroom, etc.

The data consists of so many observations, over millions of rows and tens of columns. The data was first imported to Microsoft Excel to understand the data and perform some data cleaning and data manipulation. After then, the data was saved with the columns that I will be working with, in tableau.

Analysis and Visualization

The average prices that an apartment costs in each area by zip code. 98134 area was found to be the highest and has an average price of $206.6 and the lowest price in Seattle is found in the 98125 area with an average price of $64.7.




Next, I checked the revenue for the year using a line graph. It was seen that the patronage of Airbnb apartments. Seattle experienced growth from the month of January to March, did a little drawdown, then went back up during the summer season and comes back to the level it was in March and remain consistent until the end of the year.



The last analysis I performed was to determine the total number of listings for the year. A 1-bedroom apartment has the highest listing with 1,811 and a 6-bedroom apartment has the least listing with a value of 5.



To conclude the analysis visually, I built a dashboard showcasing all the analyses performed.
